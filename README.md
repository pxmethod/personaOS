# PersonaOS

PersonaOS is an interactive workspace for exploring the people, workflows, goals, tools, and operational challenges behind modern B2B customer support organizations.

The platform is designed to help product managers, engineers, designers, operators, and leadership teams better understand how different personas interact with customer support platforms like Zendesk, Kustomer, Salesforce Service Cloud, Intercom, and similar systems.

Rather than focusing only on features, PersonaOS encourages teams to start with:
- user goals
- operational workflows
- responsibilities
- collaboration patterns
- pain points
- business outcomes

This creates stronger alignment between product strategy, customer needs, and day-to-day operational reality.

---

# Why PersonaOS Exists

Modern B2B platforms are used by many different teams:
- Support Agents
- Support Managers
- Customer Success Managers
- Support Operations
- Technical Account Managers
- AI / Automation Teams
- Sales / Account Teams
- Implementation Teams
- Engineering Escalation Teams
- Leadership

Each group:
- uses the product differently
- has different priorities
- experiences different friction
- measures success differently

PersonaOS helps teams visualize and understand those differences so product decisions can be grounded in real workflows instead of assumptions.

# Core Concepts

## Personas
A modeled representation of operational roles inside a support organization.

### Examples
- Support Agent
- Customer Success Manager
- Technical Account Manager
- Support Operations

---

## Tasks
The day-to-day operational activities performed by each persona.

### Examples
- Review queue
- Resolve escalations
- Coordinate renewals
- Build automations

---

## Features
Commonly used product surfaces and capabilities within support platforms.

### Examples
- Ticket queue
- SLA dashboards
- Workflow automations
- Customer timelines

---

## Workflow Visualizations
Sankey-style diagrams showing:
- where personas spend time
- operational workload distribution
- task concentration
- workflow complexity

---

## Persona Overlap
Analysis showing:
- shared workflows
- shared pain points
- cross-functional dependencies
- organizational overlap
